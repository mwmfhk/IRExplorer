IR Explorer

This tool allows you to sample IRs intuitively — you record a DI'd backing track and lead line as two separate tracks, after which you can, on each track, blend multiple IRs for the sound you want while the loop keeps playing.

You can then export the blending mixture of the IRs into a single IR to load into your playing rig.
